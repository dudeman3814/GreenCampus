Who We Are

Hello, Welcome to The GreenCampus initiative!
The goal of GreenCampus is to reduce environmental impact and spread awareness of this throughout the college to students and staff as well as to the people living in the local area.
We believe that it is our responsibility to take care of our environment and ensure that everyone is aware of how to reduce environmental impact and to improve sustainable living as well as encouraging eco-friendly behavior as this could help with safeguarding our environment.

Our Mission

To help carry out the goals of GreenCampus, this website will offer tips and advice on sustainability, encourage eco-friendly behavior, and act as a reliable and accessible resource for those interested in sustainable living.
We will also offer challenges related to sustainability. Also, there will be activities available for those who are interested relating to ways of reducing environmental impact.
